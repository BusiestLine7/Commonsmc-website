Game Modes

Five ways to play.

🌍
Never Resets
Skyblock Economy
The classic Skyblock experience with a full economy system. Build your island from a single block, trade with other players, and grow your balance. Your island is permanent — nothing is ever wiped.
Full Economy Player Shops Island Levelling Never Resets
💀
Never Resets
One Life
Die once and it's over. Your island is deleted and your coins are wiped — back to zero. The server itself never resets, but your run does. How long can you last? The longest survivors are immortalised in the Hall of Fame.
Hardcore One Death = Restart Hall of Fame Skyblock Economy
🧱
Never Resets
One Block
Start with a single regenerating block that drops random items with each break. Progress through phases as you collect resources and build your island from nothing. No economy — pure survival challenge.
No Economy Phased Progression Random Drops Never Resets
🤝
Never Resets
Co-op Islands
Team up with 2–4 players on a shared island with a shared economy. Build together, trade together, grow together. Perfect for friend groups who want the full Skyblock experience without going it alone.
2–4 Players Shared Island Shared Economy Never Resets
⚡
Seasonal Reset
Skyblock Rush
A seasonal race to the highest island level. Every season resets the playing field — fresh start, same goal. Winners receive a permanent Season Champion Discord role and a spot in the Hall of Fame.
Competitive Seasonal Resets Season Champion Role Hall of Fame
