Game Modes

Five ways to play.

💰
Skyblock Economy
The flagship mode. Build your island, build your empire.
Start on a small island and grow it into a thriving economy. Buy and sell resources, set up island shops, compete for the highest island value, and trade with the community. This is the core Skyblock experience on CommonsMC — with a fully player-driven economy at its heart.
Economy Island Shops Challenges Never Resets Solo or Co-op
💀
One Life
Die once. Lose everything. Start over.
The most brutal Skyblock experience. Your island runs on the Skyblock Economy — but if you die, your island is permanently deleted and your coins are wiped. Back to zero. High stakes, high reward. How long can you survive?
Hardcore Economy Island Wipes on Death Hall of Fame
🧊
One Block
One block. Infinite possibilities. Survive.
Start on a single block floating in the void. Mine it — it regenerates as a random block. Could be dirt, stone, ore, wood, or something far more rare. As you progress through phases, the blocks get more valuable and more dangerous. Build your island entirely from what the one block gives you. No economy, no hand-holding — just you, the void, and one block.
Pure Survival Random Blocks Phased Progression No Economy Never Resets
🤝
Co-op Islands
Better together. Build, trade, and thrive as a team.
Team up with 2–4 players and share one island. Pool your resources, divide tasks, and build the most valuable island on the server together. Uses the Skyblock Economy system — shared balance, shared shops, shared success.
2–4 Players Economy Shared Island Never Resets

Competitive

Skyblock Rush.

🏆 Skyblock Rush — Seasonal Competition

Skyblock Rush is the competitive layer of Skyblock. Every season, players race to reach the highest island level before the timer runs out. Rush has its own seasonal economy that wipes with each reset — giving every season a fresh start and equal footing for all players.

Season winners earn a permanent Season Champion Discord role, a spot in the Hall of Fame on the website, and exclusive in-game recognition.

  • Seasonal economy — wipes each reset
  • Race to highest island level
  • Season leaderboard tracked live
  • Season Champion Discord role
  • Hall of Fame on commonsmc.net
  • Fresh start every season

Economy

How economies work.

💰
Skyblock Economy
Shared across Skyblock Economy, One Life, and Co-op modes. Separate from Commons Survival. Island shops, trading, and challenges all feed into this economy.
🏆
Rush Economy
Skyblock Rush has its own seasonal economy that resets with each season. Keeps competition fair — everyone starts equal every season.
🧊
One Block
No economy at all. Pure survival. One Block is intentionally isolated — mine the regenerating block, build from what you get, survive. The challenge is the point.
