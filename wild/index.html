Know Before You Choose

Survival vs Wild.

Not sure which server is right for you? Here's the key difference: Commons Survival is a community-first forever world with protection and economy. Commons Wild is a raw, lawless experience where nothing is safe and nothing is forgotten.

🌿 Commons Survival

  • Land claiming — your builds are protected
  • Player economy and chest shops
  • Staff moderate the community
  • Rules enforced — griefing is banned
  • Community events and warps
  • PvP requires mutual consent
  • Forever world — safe and stable

🌋 Commons Wild

  • No claims — nothing is protected
  • No economy — pure vanilla resources
  • No staff intervention in gameplay
  • No rules — grief, raid, steal freely
  • No warps — walk or die
  • PvP always enabled everywhere
  • Forever world — raw and scarred

The Wild Promise

Commons Wild will never reset. The world you enter today will still exist years from now — battered, scarred, and full of history. Every base built, every war fought, every ruin left behind becomes part of the permanent record.

As Minecraft updates, the world grows. New biomes generate at the frontier. The old lands remember everything.
