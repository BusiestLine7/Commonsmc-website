Getting Started

Useful commands.

🏠 Homes & Teleport

  • /sethome Set your home location
  • /home Teleport to your home
  • /spawn Teleport to server spawn
  • /tpa <player> Request teleport to a player
  • /tpaccept Accept a teleport request
  • /tpdeny Deny a teleport request
  • /warp View all available warps

🛡️ Claims

  • /claim Claim land (golden shovel)
  • /abandonclaim Remove your claim
  • /trust <player> Full access to your claim
  • /containertrust <player> Chest access only
  • /accesstrust <player> Doors & buttons only
  • /untrust <player> Remove trust
  • /claimlist View all your claims

💰 Economy

  • /balance Check your balance
  • /pay <player> <amount> Pay a player
  • /warp market Visit the player market
  • /worth <item> Check item sell value

💬 Social

  • /msg <player> Private message a player
  • /reply Reply to last message
  • /discord link Link your Discord account
  • /list See online players
  • /rules View server rules
  • /helpop Contact online staff

Server Rules

Play fair. Be kind.

  1. 01 Respect all players — harassment, hate speech, and discrimination are strictly prohibited.
  2. 02 No griefing or stealing from other players' claims.
  3. 03 No cheating, hacking, or using unauthorized mods or clients.
  4. 04 No spamming or excessive caps in chat.
  5. 05 No advertising other servers.
  6. 06 Keep chat appropriate — no NSFW content.
  7. 07 No exploiting bugs or glitches — report them to staff.
  8. 08 Respect staff decisions — appeals can be made via Discord.
  9. 09 No building inappropriate structures.
  10. 10 English in main chat please — use /msg for other languages.
  11. 11 PvP is only allowed with mutual consent outside of designated zones.
