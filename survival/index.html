Commons Survival

A permanent community SMP. No resets, no pay-to-win, no nonsense — just Minecraft the way it should be.

Commons Survival is a forever world — the map will never reset. Every build, every claim, every chest shop you create becomes a permanent part of the server's history. The world only grows. It never starts over.

Features

Everything you need.

🏡
Land Claiming
Protect your builds with GriefPrevention. Claim land, subdivide plots, and trust other players into your claim.
💰
Player Economy
A fully player-driven market. Buy, sell, and trade with other players through chest shops anywhere in the world.
🏪
Admin Shop
Server-run GUI shop for floor-price selling and emergency buying. Type /shop to browse all 13 categories.
🗺️
Warps
Quick travel to spawn, the wild, and the nether. The spawn area doubles as the market hub.
🌙
Sleep Voting
Only 30% of online players need to sleep to skip the night. No more waiting for that one player.
🔗
Discord Integration
In-game chat bridged to Discord, join/leave notifications, death messages, and a live server status bot.
🏆
Leaderboards
Kill/death rankings and longest survival records tracked and displayed in Discord with /leaderboard.
🛡️
No Pay to Win
Zero gameplay advantages for sale. Ever. Ranks are earned through playtime and trust, not money.
⚡
Optimised Performance
PaperMC with ClearLag and tuned settings. The server is built to stay fast even with lots of players online.

Commands

Get around quickly.

🏠 Homes & Travel

  • /sethome Set your home point
  • /home Teleport to your home
  • /warp spawn Teleport to spawn
  • /warp wild Random teleport into the wild
  • /warp nether Teleport to the nether hub
  • /tpa <player> Request teleport to a player
  • /tpaccept Accept a teleport request

💰 Economy

  • /balance Check your coin balance
  • /pay <player> <amount> Send coins
  • /sell hand Sell the item you're holding
  • /sell all Sell your entire inventory
  • /worth Check an item's floor price
  • /shop Open the admin GUI shop

🏪 Shops & Claims

  • /qs help QuickShop help menu
  • /qs find <item> Find nearby player shops
  • /claimlist View your claims
  • /trust <player> Trust a player in your claim
  • /abandonclaim Delete a claim

💬 Social

  • /msg <player> Send a private message
  • /reply Reply to last message
  • /list See who is online
  • /helpop <message> Message staff
  • /discord link Link your Discord account

Rules

Keep it fair.

01
No griefing. Don't destroy, steal from, or interfere with other players' builds or claims.
02
No hacking or cheating. No clients that give gameplay advantages. No x-ray, no fly hacks, no auto-clickers.
03
No harassment. Keep chat respectful. Targeted harassment, hate speech, and excessive toxicity will result in a mute or ban.
04
No scamming. Trade fairly. Misleading shop descriptions, bait-and-switch deals, and dishonest trading are not allowed.
05
No advertising. Don't advertise other servers or Discord servers in chat.
06
No lag machines. Don't build machines intentionally designed to cause server lag. Staff will remove them without warning.
